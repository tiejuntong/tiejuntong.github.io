     The B5 Club (套娃五兄弟)


    Finally, just for fun, there are 5 prestigious journals in statistics and related areas starting with the B letter including Bernoulli, Bioinformatics, Biometrics, Biometrika, and Biostatistics. From one perspective, these 5 journals cover all aspects ranging from probability, statistics to biostatistics. From another perspective, these 5 journals cover all aspects ranging from theory, methodology to application.

    I now name them as "The B5 Club". To the best of my limited knowledge, there are only 9 statisticians in the world eligible for this club (i.e., who has published articles in all these journals) as follows.

    1.   Mark van der Laan, University of California at Berkeley, eligible in 2002
